Why PIN exists

Identity on the web is fragmented. Your information is scattered across platforms you don't control, locked in proprietary formats, and tied to services that might disappear. PIN fixes this.

We believe identity is foundational infrastructure. Like DNS or email, it should be reliable, portable, and under your control. PIN gives you a canonical source of truth for who you are—on your own terms.

🔒 Own Your Data

No cloud vendor lock-in. Your identity runs on your server with zero external dependencies. Full control, full privacy.

👥 Multi-Profile Ready

Manage individuals, teams, and organizations from one system. Perfect for personal brands or company identity infrastructure.

🛡 Privacy Controls

Decide what's public and what stays private. Control field-level visibility for each profile. Share selectively—your way.

🎨 Full Customization

Design your identity. Choose from bold themes, upload custom CSS, and make your profile truly yours.

🌐 Portable Identity

Export to JSON, XML, vCard, or TXT. Use the same identity data everywhere—your website, your tools, your automations.

🖼 Profile Picture Export

Upload your profile picture and export it in any size. Perfect for avatars, headers, or high-res prints. Control your visual identity everywhere.

🚀 AT Protocol Compatible

Publish your AT handle and DID, plus resolve them at /.well-known/atproto-did for seamless federation.

🤖 API + MCP Ready

Serve identity data to agents and automations with predictable endpoints and a built-in MCP interface.
